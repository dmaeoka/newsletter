Client updates

Anousha's announcements from Motability

Our milestone for this month included completing the development for Release 23. The release includes GDPR enhancements, audit capabilities for Wordpress, and other account-related changes to support our Google maps functionalities across the websites.

The team also did the groundwork and development to make some mega menu enhancements, but due to some adverse impact on the sites, the changes needed to be rolled back. The show goes on however; both internal and external teams are supporting each other to get through UAT, and then the production release, in the next couple of weeks.

In other news, we have also started planning for Release 24, and have begun some very early discussions on the pipeline for Release 25 and beyond. More to follow on this next month.

Andrew's updates from BAE Hawk

The team - Matteo, Diego, Richard, Marta, Karunakar - has now introduced the Identity Cloud Service to production. The solution was first discussed over eighteen months ago, and it has taken a lot of perseverance from everyone involved with Hawk to finally get the job done. The implementation offers another significant improvement in BAE's portal capability, with self-service for users on password management and account access, two-factor authentication for additional security, and single sign-on across portal and identity cloud among many of the new features introduced.
